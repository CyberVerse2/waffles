# Waffles — World Cup 2026 Campaign Plan

## 1 · Executive summary

The World Cup is a six-week window where hundreds of millions of people care about the same thing at the same time, every day. Trivia is the lowest-friction way to convert that attention into daily, repeatable actions for our users. Waffles will turn that attention into hourly live games themed on the world cup and prizes.

### Match of the Hour — live tournaments

Every hour, thousands of players face the same set of football questions at the same time. Answer fast for more points; standings are provisional until the round closes, then scores lock and prizes settle. One entry per round keeps it fair and creates a clean, repeatable appointment to return.

### Football question formats

Beyond classic multiple-choice, the World Cup pack uses richer formats that suit football:

- **Pick-the-winners** — select the correct set (e.g. nations that have won the Cup).
- **Ordering** — arrange winners or hosts into chronological order.
- **Tap-the-flag / map** — spatial questions on a board of national flags.
- **Trivia bingo** — tap every true statement.
- **Classic timed** — fast one-of-four for pace.

you can find all question formats here: https://wafflesv2.vercel.app/world-cup

Content spans winners, hosts,moments, iconic players, and records — refreshed daily and is subtly dynamic to mirror the actual world cup calendary.

### Solo level path

Between live rounds, players climb a World Cup-themed solo leveling path at their own pace. It's the free practice ground and where players practice and get rewarded with tickets to join tournaments.
